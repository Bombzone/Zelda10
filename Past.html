The Legend of Zelda A Link to the Past is the third Legend of Zelda game made. It was released in the US on April 13, 1992. It was originaly for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES), which is the US version of the Super Famicon. It has also been rerelesed for many systems. In addition it also has another game in its world Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds.
